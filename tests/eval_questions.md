# Evaluation Questions

Use 8 to 15 questions derived from the three provided documents. Suggested review points:

1. Does retrieval surface the correct chunk(s)?
2. Does the answer stay within the provided evidence?
3. Does the answer mention when context is missing?
4. Is the answer complete and concise?

Starter prompts:

1. What factors affect construction project delays?
2. Which policy rules apply to vendor onboarding?
3. What documents are required for compliance review?
4. When should an issue be escalated?
5. What are the service level commitments?
6. Which constraints or exclusions are explicitly mentioned?
7. What approval steps are required before work begins?
8. What information must a contractor provide to support a claim?
